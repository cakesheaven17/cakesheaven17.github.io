My Story

Cake's Heaven was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to create beautiful, delicious cakes that make every occasion special. I, Sheikh Hasina, the founder, started baking in my home kitchen, and through word of mouth, my creations quickly became the talk of the town.

Today, I am proud to have a team of talented bakers and decorators who continue the tradition of crafting handcrafted cakes with love and attention to detail. We use only the finest ingredients, sourcing locally whenever possible.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, wedding, or simply want to treat yourself, we would love to create something special just for you!
